Featured Artists

Jax "Brick" Monroe

Jax "Brick" Monroe is a dynamic urban artist known for transforming gritty cityscapes into vibrant canvases with his captivating spray paint murals. Born and raised in the heart of Brooklyn, New York, Jax discovered his passion for street art at a young age, blending influences from hip-hop culture, graffiti, and modern art.

With a spray can in one hand and a vision in the other, Jax takes to the streets, seeking out weathered brick walls and forgotten alleyways to breathe new life into them. His signature style combines bold colors and intricate patterns, often telling stories about community, resilience, and the urban experience. Jax believes that art should be accessible to everyone, and he strives to create pieces that resonate with passersby, inviting them to pause and reflect on the beauty around them.

Mikael Outlaw

Mikael Outlaw is an innovative urban artist who has captured the essence of city life through his stunning spray paint art adorning the walls of businesses and community spaces. Raised in the culturally rich neighborhoods of Minneapolis, Mikael's artistic journey began as a kid, sketching in notebooks and experimenting with paint on any surface he could find.

Mikael's work is characterized by a vibrant color palette and a unique blend of realism and abstract stylization. His murals often depict themes of unity, empowerment, and the everyday stories of individuals within the community. Believing that art has the power to connect people, Mikael strives to create pieces that resonate emotionally, encouraging viewers to engage with their surroundings and appreciate the beauty in the ordinary.

Over the years, Mikael has collaborated with local businesses, schools, and community organizations to transform blank walls into lively displays of creativity and culture. His murals have beautified everything from coffee shops to community centers, turning overlooked spaces into landmarks that spark conversations and inspire pride in the neighborhood.
